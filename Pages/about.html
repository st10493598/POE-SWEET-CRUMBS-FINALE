Founded in 2019 by Lufuno, Sweet Crumbs Bakery started as a small kitchen business selling cupcakes and muffins. It gained popularity and opened a physical storefront within a year. Known for homemade recipes and custom cakes, it has become a beloved local spot.

To bring joy and connection to the community through freshly baked goods made with quality ingredients and passion.

To become Thohoyandou's most trusted bakery by offering exceptional baked goods, expanding delivery services, and enhancing online presence.
